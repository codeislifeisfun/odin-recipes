Celerita Recipe


Thirst quenching drink

Ingredients:

  • 4 Tbsps chopped celery
  • 1 tsp white sugar
  • 3 oz tequila
  • 12 fluid oz lime-flavored seltzer water

Steps:

  1. Muddle celery & sugar in a mixing glass until crushed
  2. Add tequila & stir to dissolve sugar
  3. Strain into 2 ice-filled Colins or rocks glasses
  4. Top with seltzer
