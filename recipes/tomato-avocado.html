Tomato-avocado Recipe


Start your day right

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 avocado -peeled, pitted, & mashed
  • 1 tsp garlic salt
  • 1 slice ciabatta bread
  • 3 slices tomato
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Mix mashed avocado & garlic salt in a bowl
  2. Spread on ciabatta bread
  3. Layer tomatoes over avocado & top with black pepper
