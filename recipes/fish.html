Special Fish Soup


Light & filling

Ingredients:

  • 1 two-headed fish from river filled with chemicals
  • 2 liters water from chemical river
  • 2 cubes shrimp flavor
  • 2 medium red seaweed
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • salt & pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Boil water in a large pot & dissolve broth cubes
  2. Place fish once the cubes dissolve & cook for 10 minutes
  3. Reduce heat to low, stir in seaweed, carrots, & onions cook for 10 minutes
  4. Add salt & pepper to taste
