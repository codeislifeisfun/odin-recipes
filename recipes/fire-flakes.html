Super Fire Flakes


Start your day right

Ingredients:

  • 20 pcs freshly harvested ghost pepper
  • 5 Tbsps of hot chilli oil
  • 1 Tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 head garlic
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Heat cooking oil in wok
  2. Add garlic once the oil becomes hot
  3. Add ghost pepper, once garlic becomes fragrant, & split into two
  4. Remove ghost peper & garlic once your eyes start to burn
  5. Add chilli oil, black pepper, & salt
