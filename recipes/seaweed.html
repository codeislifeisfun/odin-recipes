Seaweed Noodles


Comfort food for the broken hearted

Ingredients:

  • 800 grams kombu
  • 2 liters of fish or clam stock
  • 4 Tbsps chopped celery
  • 1 tsp white sugar
  • 3 oz tequila

Steps:

  1. Boil 2 liters of fish or clam stock in a pot
  2. Add 800 grams of kombu into pot
  3. Muddle celery & sugar in a mixing glass until crushed
  4. Remove pot from burner once kombu turns bright green
  5. Let cool for 5 minutes then add celery-sugar mix
  6. Add tequila
