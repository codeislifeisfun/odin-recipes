Veggie-Pesto Soup Recipe


Light & filling

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups water
  • 6 cubes vegetable broth
  • 2 medium potatoes, cubed
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1 large zucchini, cubed
  • 2 Tbsps sun-dried tomato pesto

Steps:

  1. Boil water in a large pot & dissolve broth cubes
  2. Place potatoes, carrots, onion, zucchini into pot; cook for 10 minutes
  3. Reduce heat to low, stir in sun-dried tomato pesto
  4. Simmer for 35 minutes or until potatoes are very tender
